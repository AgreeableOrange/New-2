Fried egg

How to fry an egg :)

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Take a pan and beat it up
  2. Pour some oil(around a tablespoon)
  3. Crack an egg in a glass and mix with spices
  4. Pour the mixture of egg in pan and heat it up
  5. Flip the egg after a minute and turn off the heat after a minute
  6. Serve it and enjoy
